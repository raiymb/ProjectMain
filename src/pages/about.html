Our Company

Our Heritage

Once upon a time, nestled in the heart of Shymkent, there was a quaint and cozy cafe named "Derbes". This charming cafe was famous throughout the city for serving the best coffee in town, and its owner, Raiymbek Batyr, was the mastermind behind this caffeinated oasis.
Derbes, a word that held special significance for Raiymbek, was his own ancestral home. He had decided to name the cafe after it, as a tribute to his roots and the history of Shymkent. Raiymbek had a deep love for his city and its rich cultural heritage, and he wanted Derbes to reflect that love.

Our Coffee

The moment you stepped into Derbes, you were greeted by the inviting aroma of freshly brewed coffee beans and the warm smiles of the cafe's friendly staff. The interior was a blend of modern and traditional design, with wooden tables and chairs, cozy couches, and artwork depicting the history of Shymkent adorning the walls.
But what truly set Derbes apart was its coffee. Raiymbek was passionate about coffee and had traveled far and wide to learn the art of coffee roasting and brewing. He sourced the finest coffee beans from around the world and meticulously roasted them to perfection, creating a unique blend that was a closely guarded secret. Each cup of coffee served at Derbes was a masterpiece, and customers often marveled at the rich, aromatic, and perfectly balanced brew.
However, Derbes wasn't just about coffee. Raiymbek wanted to celebrate the city's history as a hub for tea production, which dated back to ancient times. In addition to their incredible coffee, Derbes also offered a diverse selection of exquisite teas from Shymkent and Middle Eastern countries. The tea menu included everything from traditional black and green teas to aromatic herbal infusions and rare blends sourced from far-flung tea gardens.

Our Events

As word spread about Derbes, the cafe became a gathering place for locals and tourists alike. It wasn't just a place to enjoy exceptional beverages; it was a place to connect with the city's culture and history. Raiymbek often hosted cultural events, live music performances, and art exhibitions, turning Derbes into a vibrant cultural hub.

Our Partners

We like to say that we are not in the coffee business serving people, but in the people business serving coffee. Our employees – who we call partners – are at the heart of the Derbes experience. We are committed to making our partners proud and investing in their health, well-being and success and to creating a culture of belonging where everyone is welcome.

Our Values

As it has been from the beginning, our purpose goes far beyond profit. We believe Derbes can, and should, have a positive impact on the communities we serve.

People

Our aspiration is to be people positive – investing in humanity and the well-being of everyone we connect with, from our partners to coffee farmers to the customers in our stores and beyond.

Planet

We are striving to become resource positive – giving back more than we take from the planet. We are working to store more carbon than we emit, replenish more freshwater than we use, and eliminate waste. We know we can’t do it alone. It takes all of us.
